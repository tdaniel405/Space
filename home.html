Computer Information Systems



Information Systems

Professionals serve as strategic liaisons between

Administrative and Technological functions of an organization.

I.S. Professionals integrate business processes and complex information technology while serving as consultants,

designers, or implementers of new solutions that assist an organization in achieving goals.

Related occupations include Information Systems Manager, Chief Information Officer,

Project Manager, Computer Systems Analyst, and Systems Administrator.
